Seafoam Pudding

Seafoam Pudding is a cooked dish that you prepare in the kitchen of an upgraded farmhouse or Cookout Kit after reaching Fishing Level 9, purchasing one at the shop at the Dance of the Moonlight Jellies event or purchasing it from the Traveling Cart.

Note: For this recipe we will assume that you have already reached level 9 fishing & have an upgraded farmhouse as this is considered a late game recipe

Ingredients

  • Flounder
  • Midnight Carp
  • Squid Ink

Instructions

  1. First, you will need to catch a flounder, this can be caught in the spring & summer from 6AM to 8PM
  2. Second, you will need to obtain Squid Ink which can be obtained by either killing a Squid Kid or obtaining it from any kind of squid residing in a fish pond
  3. Third, you will need to catch a midnight carp. These can be caught in any sort of fresh body of water in fall or winter at 10PM to 2AM
  4. Lastly, you will need to go to the kitchen and make the dish
