Fried Egg Recipe

A Fried Egg is a cooked dish that can be made either via an upgraded farmhouse or a Cookout Kit

Note:For this Recipe we will assume that you have a Coop with chickens & an upgraded farmhouse

Ingredients

  • Egg

Instructions

  1. First, you will obtain an egg layed by a chicken in a coop
  2. Last, you will make the dish in the kitchen in your upgraded farmhouse
