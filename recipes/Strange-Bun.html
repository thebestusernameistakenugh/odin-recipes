Strange Bun Recipe

A strange bun is a cooked dish made in the upgraded farmhouse after obtaining the recipe for it from Shane in the mail after you have gained seven hearts with him. Strange Buns can also be dropped by a Shadow Brute, in the Stardrop Saloon's trash & it will occasionally be in stock at the Traveling Cart

Note: For this recipe we will already assume that you not only have seven hearts with Shane but you also have not only seven hearts with shane but you're also past or near the end of the game as this is considered a late game item

Ingredients

  • Wheat Flour
  • Periwinkle
  • Void Mayonnaise

Instructions

  1. First, you will harvest Wheat, which grows in the Summer & Fall & you will then put it's contents in a Mill
  2. Second, you will obtain a Periwinkle which can be caught using a crab pot placed in freshwater
  3. Third, you will need to obtain Void Mayonnaise which comes from putting a Void Chicken egg in a Mayonnaise Making Machine
  4. Last, you will need to have the ingredients in your inventory & craft the item at your farmhouse kitchen
